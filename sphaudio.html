Description

In the Sphaudio visualizer application, audio waveform data is mapped to spherical balls. Each ball corresponds to a specific frequency range and bounces up and down according to the average amplitude of the nodes sampled in their corresponding frequency ranges. The visualizers for the web and mobile application are oriented in slightly different ways although their underlying logic is nearly identical. In the mobile application, the balls are oriented in three dimensions in which there are nxn spheres customizable to 7x7 and 5x5 spheres corresponding to 49 and 25 frequency ranges respectively. In the web application, there are 40 spheres aligned horizontally corresponding to 40 frequency ranges. Both web and mobile applications allow for custom color schemes. In the mobile application, the color of the ball at any given point in time is also dependent on its amplitude. In the web application, there also exist rectangular markers at each frequency range that animate up and down with its corresponding ball.
