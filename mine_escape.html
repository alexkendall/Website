Documentation

Free Play

Select this to enter the level menu which will reveal and give you access to all 125 levels. There are five difficulties, EASY (4x4), MEDIUM (5x5), HARD (6x6), INSANE(7x7), and IMPOSSIBLE (8x8). You may select any level here in the level menu although we recommend you complete the levels in order. Furthemore, there are progress indicators for each levels. 3 green bars indicates the level is complete, 2 yellow bars indicates the level has almost been completed, and 1 red bar indicates that the level has been attempted with little success. To select a level, one simply taps the corresponding level button in the menu and that level will be loaded. Furthemore, there is a time button in the bottom right corner of the level menu. The time button allows one to view the best time for each level. Simply click the time key and you will have access to the best times for each level. Likewise, selecting a level after entering the times menu will reveal the best time for that corresponding level, and incomplete if that level has yet to be completed.

Settings

To change the game volume, simply click the + button to increment the volume and the - button to decrement the volume. Clicking restore progress will give you the option to clear all of your game data and level progress.

Achievements

Assuming one has Game Center enabled on their device, they will have access to achievements for the game. The achievements page allows one to view their progress towards both incompleted and completed achievements.

Playing the Game

You begin the game by selecting the top left corner of the map. Your objective is to select each location not containing a mine. After you select that location, it will turn grey. Likewise, if a mine appears in a location, you should never select that square. After a mine pops up, it will be visible for a short amount of time, and then disappear. However, even after the mine disappears, it still exists at that location. You lose the game if you select a location in which a mine was once visible. You win the game if you select each location for which no mine exists. There are two types of mines, red mines, and blue mines. Red mines are visible for a greater amount of time than blue mines. The color of the mine indicates how much longer it will be visible. A red mine will flash from black to red, to orange, and then finally to yellow and disappear. A blue mine will flash from black to dark blue, to royal blue, and to light blue before it becomes invisible. As the difficulty increases, you will see more blue mines and less red ones. At the bottom of the map, you have the option to go back a level, repeat a level, and skip to the next level. If you win a game, a window will appear that will show you your time and success. It will prompt you to the next level if you won, or to repeat the level if you failed.
